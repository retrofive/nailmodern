Our Story

A Haven of Beauty & Relaxation

Nestled in the heart of Marco Island, Modern Nails & Spa offers the ultimate in pampering and natural beauty enhancement. Our wholehearted, creative, and professional staff are dedicated to an experience that's truly yours.

We believe a nail salon should be more than a quick stop — it should be a sanctuary. That's why we prioritize your comfort, well-being, and health above all. From the moment you step in, you'll feel the difference that passion and professionalism make.

We use only the most trusted brands in the beauty industry, bringing you the latest advances in nail care, pedicures, spa treatments, and beyond.
